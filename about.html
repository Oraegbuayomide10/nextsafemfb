Board of Directors

ORAEGBU PHILIP

Chief Excecutive Officer

He is known as a multifaceted, imaginative business leader, and his career path has spanned from corporate communication and journalism to finance today.  With his current venture into microfinance banking, he is moving closer to realizing his goal of creating a financial super market that will affect everyone in Africa and Nigeria.

He joined the company in 2003 from THISDAY Newspaper, where he worked his way up from the Financial Correspondent in 1995 to Deputy Editor of the Saturday paper in 2003. He left the company in 2009 as the head of communications.

After completing his schooling at the University of Jos and his service in Kano State, He started his career as a journalist in 1993 at The Guardian. He has been able to accomplish the unthinkable in the shortest amount of time by using leadership concepts as the center of business organization built around the ideals of honesty and a dedication to excellence.

ADEBAYO COLLINS

Chief Operating Officer

Mr Collins is a chartered Accountant and an associate member of the Institute of Chartered Accountant of Nigeria (ICAN), qualifying in the year 1992. He presently serves as NextSave Cooperative Finance and Investment Society Ltd.'s Chief of Operating Officer. He has held the position of Chief Accountant for a number of companies, including Thomas Kingsley Securities Ltd., Power and Communications Nigeria Limited, HIRAN Nigeria Ltd., and MUNTARI DANGANA & Co.

He is a skilled accountant with years of experience who has contributed his knowledge and skills to the Bank..

ORAEGBU NIYI

Managing Director - Portharcourt Branch

Oraegbu Niyi earned a First Class Honours degree from the University of Nigeria, Nsukka (UNN) in 2010 and won the faculty and departmental awards for being the top student.

With almost ten years of expertise, he is an accomplished banker. He has held middle and senior management positions in many indigenous banks' SME desks as well as Retail and Consumer Banking departments during his banking career.

Mr. Niyi is very analytical, cerebral, and focused on getting things done. He has a solid background in risk management, project management microfinancing, as well as strategic management, leadership, capacity building, and customer relationship management. Additionally, he participates in numerous mentoring and career development initiatives for young people. He holds a master's degree from the University of Sydney as well other professional qualifications.
