Save

Saving money is a wise decision, but discipline is a quality that most people lack. We're automating saving as a result of technology. You specify a savings goal, and we help take care of the rest, including timely interest payments. So no matter what your monthly income is, you can set aside a part of it for the future without having to worry about forgetting or having too little money to put away.

Invest

With fractional shares on NextSave, we're making trusted international investment options more accessible to every African, giving anyone with a NextSave account the chance to grow their money anywhere in the world. We believe that investing should be made available to everyone, and our mission is to ensure that Africans can take advantage of the same financial opportunities as their peers across the world.

Loan

When you need one, you can get a quick loan. Just 0.3% daily interest; no embarrassment. You can borrow money for a short period of time through NextSave Overdrafts. Interest rates are low, and you can borrow as much or as little money as you need for any purpose, giving you the financial freedom to tackle unexpected bills or take advantage of opportunities quickly.
